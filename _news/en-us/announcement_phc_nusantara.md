We are thrilled to announce that our collaborative research project has been awarded funding through the **PHC Nusantara 2024** program, a France-Indonesia governmental initiative facilitated by Campus France.

#### Project Overview

**Title:** *Improving the Self-care Independence of Stroke Survivors through Active Community Participation by utilizing Smartwatch and Mobile App*

**Duration:** 2024-2026

**Partners:**
- Muhammadiyah University of Yogyakarta, Indonesia
- Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS), France

#### My Role

As **Co-researcher**, I contribute to:
- Speech-to-thematic coding implementation & analysis
- Developing qualitative coding frameworks
- Data analysis and statistical modeling
- Preparing research publications

This project combines my expertise in audio-visual perception with healthcare technology to improve stroke rehabilitation outcomes through accessible digital tools.
